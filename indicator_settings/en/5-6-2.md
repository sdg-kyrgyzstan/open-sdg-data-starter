### [Law of the Kyrgyz Republic of 12 January 2024 No. 14 "On the Protection of Citizens’ Health in the Kyrgyz Republic"](https://cbd.minjust.gov.kg/4-5260/edition/1939/ru)
#### Article 6. State policy in the field of health protection, paragraph 16
#### Article 57. Reproductive rights of citizens
#### Article 61. Rights of children and minors, paragraph 2
#### § 4. Issues of reproductive health and the protection of maternal and child health

#### The law recognizes sexual and reproductive rights of citizens as an integral part of human rights, defines their content, and establishes guarantees for their realization. The law also provides for state guarantees and establishes a legal framework for accessing services related to the protection of sexual and reproductive health of the citizens of the Kyrgyz Republic.
